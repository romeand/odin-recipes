Molletes

Description

When it comes to staple foods Molletes definitely has to be as staple of a food as there can be. Mostly available as a breakfast dish in Spain and Mexico. Although this simple platter is quick to make, it is warmingly fulfilling, thus its popularity

Already rich in taste, it is an amazing gift from the universe and the powers that be that we can actually improve this delicacy adding a custom made Pico de galloto the top of our home made molletes.

Lastly, we musn't forget that the cheese selection for our molletes allows us to change things up every now and then, so be sure to keep handy different fresh cheese bricks cus you never know when a molletes craving will strike, and believe me, that can happen anytime anywhere.

Ingredients

  • 3 bolillos.
  • Half a can of mashed beens.
  • 250gm of mennonite cheese slices.
  • 2 diced tomatoes.
  • Half onion diced.
  • 1 bunch of disinfected cilantro.

Steps

  1. Cut the fresh bolillos in half horizontally.
  2. Spread the mashed beans on the bollillos evenly.
  3. Place the cheese slices on the top side of the bolillo covering the beans.
  4. Bake in the oven for about 5 minutes at the 'toast' setting or around 250 CÂ° until the cheese melts.
  5. Take out the molletes and add the pico de gallo on top.
    • *If desired you may add 1 or 2 diced jalapenos to the pico de gallo for extra spice!
  6. Enjoy and provecho!
